Why PrepPulse?

PrepPulse is a placement readiness self-assessment platform that helps students evaluate their preparation across DSA, Core CS, Aptitude, Projects, and Communication. Based on user responses, it provides category-wise analysis, personalized recommendations, and a readiness report to support continuous improvement. The assessment is intended to guide learning and preparation rather than predict placement outcomes.
